History of UMT

The Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) started as a branch of Universiti Pertanian Malaysia. Over time, it evolved into a full-fledged university renowned nationally and internationally.

Vision & Mission

UMT’s vision is to be a marine-focused university, reputed nationally and respected globally. Our mission is to generate knowledge for the prosperity of the community and sustainable development.
